Before I ask you anything, I want you to understand what you are walking into.

Numbers are not divination. They are God's original language, embedded in creation before any human religion existed. Your name, your date of birth, your location. These are not accidents. They are coordinates. And I am here to help you read them.

God Uses Numbers, The Biblical Record
Psalm 90:12
KJV"So teach us to number our days, that we may apply our hearts unto wisdom."
YLT"To number our days, so cause us to know, And we bring in a heart of wisdom."
ESV"So teach us to number our days that we may get a heart of wisdom."
God commands us to count. Numbering is not optional. It is the path to wisdom.
Daniel 5:25–26
KJV"MENE, MENE, TEKEL, UPHARSIN… God hath numbered thy kingdom, and finished it."
YLT"MENE: God hath numbered thy kingdom, and finished it."
ESV"MENE: God has numbered the days of your kingdom and brought it to an end."
God calculated and decoded a king's entire reign through a number. Numbers carry verdict and verdict carries destiny.
Matthew 10:30
KJV"But the very hairs of your head are all numbered."
YLT"But of you, even the hairs of the head are all numbered."
ESV"But even the hairs of your head are all numbered."
God is still counting. He counts what others dismiss as uncountable. Nothing about you is unnumbered.
Psalm 147:4
KJV"He telleth the number of the stars; he calleth them all by their names."
YLT"Numbering the stars by count, to all of them He calleth names."
ESV"He determines the number of the stars; he gives to all of them their names."
God numbers and names simultaneously. Your number and your name are bound together before God.
John 21:11
KJV"Simon Peter went up, and drew the net to land full of great fishes, an hundred and fifty and three."
YLT"Simon Peter went up, and drew the net to the land, full of great fishes, a hundred and fifty and three."
ESV"Simon Peter hauled the net ashore, full of large fish, 153 of them."
153 fish. The Holy Spirit recorded the exact number. God never just says "many." He says the number because numbers carry meaning.
Revelation 13:18
KJV"Here is wisdom. Let him that hath understanding count the number…"
YLT"Here is the wisdom! He who is having the understanding, let him count the number…"
ESV"This calls for wisdom: let the one who has understanding calculate the number…"
Wisdom and the ability to count are inseparable in Scripture. Aligna operates from this exact command.
Numbers 1:1–3
KJV"Take ye the sum of all the congregation of the children of Israel… thou and Aaron shall number them."
YLT"Take ye up the sum of all the company of the sons of Israel… thou dost number them."
ESV"Take a census of all the congregation of the people of Israel… you and Aaron shall list them."
The entire fourth book of the Bible is called Numbers because God commanded a census. He numbered His people by name, by tribe, by family. Your number is your tribe.
Isaiah 40:26
KJV"Lift up your eyes on high, and behold who hath created these things, that bringeth out their host by number."
YLT"Lift up on high your eyes, and see, who hath prepared these? He who is bringing out by number their host."
ESV"Lift up your eyes on high and see: who created these? He who brings out their host by number."
Creation was deployed by number. Stars, seasons, days, all brought forth numerically.
Ecclesiastes 3:1
KJV"To every thing there is a season, and a time to every purpose under the heaven."
YLT"For everything there is an appointed time, and a time for every purpose under the heavens."
ESV"For everything there is a season, and a time for every matter under heaven."
Your season has a number. Your purpose has a time. Aligna reads both.
1 Chronicles 12:32
KJV"And of the children of Issachar, which were men that had understanding of the times, to know what Israel ought to do."
YLT"And of the sons of Issachar, having understanding for the times, to know what Israel ought to do."
ESV"Of Issachar, men who had understanding of the times, to know what Israel ought to do."
The sons of Issachar could read the numbers of their era. This is the same gift being activated in you.

Proverbs 4:7 says wisdom is the principal thing. Hosea 4:6 says God's people perish for lack of knowledge. What you are about to receive is not witchcraft. It is the knowledge of your season, your assignment, and the angels God has assigned to walk with you.

This is Word-based. Every number traces back to scripture. Every insight comes with a verse. You are not consulting the stars. You are consulting the Author of the stars, through the intelligence He embedded in the fabric of your life.

You are responsible to be in alignment. This is not passive. It is an act of will, faith, and stewardship.
